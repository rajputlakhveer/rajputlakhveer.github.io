# 🔍 What is MetaCognition?

**MetaCognition = Awareness + Control of your own thinking process**

In simple words:

* 🧠 Knowing *how you think*
* 🎯 Controlling *how you learn and act*

It has **2 core components**:

### 1. 🧠 MetaCognitive Knowledge

Understanding:

* What you know 🤔
* What you don’t know ❓
* How you learn best 📚

### 2. 🎮 MetaCognitive Regulation

The ability to:

* Plan 📝
* Monitor 👀
* Evaluate 📊

---

# 🧬 The Psychology Behind Genius

## 🔹 1. The “Self-Awareness Loop”

Genius people constantly ask:

* “Am I thinking correctly?”
* “Is there a better way?”

This creates a loop:
👉 Think → Reflect → Improve → Repeat

---

## 🔹 2. The “Error Detection System”

Highly intelligent individuals:

* Detect mistakes faster ⚡
* Adapt quickly 🔄

> *“Anyone who has never made a mistake has never tried anything new.”* — Albert Einstein

---

## 🔹 3. The “Cognitive Flexibility Principle”

They don’t stick to one belief.

Instead:

* 🔁 Shift perspectives
* 🧩 Connect ideas
* 💡 Think in systems

---

## 🔹 4. The “Deliberate Thinking Effect”

Unlike autopilot thinkers:

* They slow down 🐢
* Question assumptions ❗
* Think deeply 🔬

# 🚀 How to Use MetaCognition to Achieve ANYTHING

## 🎯 Step 1: Plan Like a Strategist

Before starting anything, ask:

* What is my goal?
* What strategy will I use?

👉 Example:
Instead of “I will study coding”
➡️ “I will learn Ruby by building 1 project in 7 days”

---

## 👀 Step 2: Monitor Like a Scientist

While working:

* Am I understanding this?
* Am I distracted?

👉 Use:

* Pomodoro ⏱️
* Self-questioning ❓

---

## 📊 Step 3: Evaluate Like a Coach

After completing:

* What worked?
* What failed?

👉 Example:
After writing code:

* Was it clean?
* Could it be optimized?

---

## 🔁 Step 4: Iterate Relentlessly

Success = Iteration 🔄

> *“We are what we repeatedly do.”* — Aristotle

# 🧱 Habit Stacking for MetaCognition (POWERFUL 🔥)

Habit stacking = Attach new habits to existing ones

---

## 🪜 Stack 1: Morning Thinking Ritual

☀️ After waking up:

* Write 3 goals ✍️
* Ask: *Why are these important?*

---

## 🪜 Stack 2: Learning Loop

📚 While studying:

* After every 25 min:

  * Ask: *What did I just learn?*

---

## 🪜 Stack 3: Reflection Journal

🌙 Before sleep:

* What did I do well? ✅
* What can I improve? 🔧

---

## 🪜 Stack 4: Decision Audit

Before big decisions:

* What assumptions am I making?
* What could go wrong?
