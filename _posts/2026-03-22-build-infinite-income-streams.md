* Freelancing / Consulting 💻

## 🧠 Psychological Principles of Wealth Creation

### 1. 🕰️ Delayed Gratification

Rich people sacrifice today for tomorrow.

* Skip instant pleasure → Build long-term assets

👉 Think: *“Will this decision pay me later?”*

---

### 2. 🔁 Compounding Effect

Small efforts daily → Massive results over time

* ₹500/day invested = Lakhs in a few years 💥
* 1 blog/day = Traffic + passive income

---

### 3. 🧩 Ownership Mindset

Don’t just work for money → **Own systems that generate money**

* Job = Linear income
* Assets = Exponential income

---

### 4. 🎯 Focused Attention

Scattered efforts = Weak income
Focused execution = Strong streams

👉 One stream at a time → Then scale

---

### 5. 🧘‍♂️ Identity Shift

Start thinking:

> “I am a person who creates income streams”

Your identity shapes your results 🔥
