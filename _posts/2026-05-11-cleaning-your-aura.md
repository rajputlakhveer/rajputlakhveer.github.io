# 🌿 How to Cleanse Your Aura

## 🧘 1. Meditation

Meditation removes emotional noise.

Even 10–15 minutes daily can:

* Reduce anxiety
* Improve clarity
* Increase emotional control

### Beginner Practice

1. Sit quietly
2. Close eyes
3. Focus on breathing
4. Observe thoughts without reacting

---

## 🌬️ 2. Breathwork

Breathing deeply calms the nervous system.

Try:

* Box Breathing
* Wim Hof breathing
* Deep diaphragmatic breathing

### Simple Technique

Inhale for 4 seconds
Hold for 4 seconds
Exhale for 4 seconds
Repeat 🔁

---

## 🌞 3. Spend Time in Nature

Nature resets emotional overload.

### Benefits:

* Reduces stress
* Increases calmness
* Improves creativity
* Restores mental energy

Even a 20-minute walk can shift your energy dramatically 🌳

Journaling releases trapped emotional energy.
